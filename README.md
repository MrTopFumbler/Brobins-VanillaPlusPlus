# Brobin's Vanilla++ (Beta)
A simple, vanilla-like modpack for NeoForge Minecraft 1.21.1<br>
It is designed around exploration and building, and is filled with fun gameplay additions and QoL features.<br>
Feel free to create forks or suggest additions :)<br>

**This modpack was created for fun as a quick project, everything is subject to a lot of change.**<br>
**It will not and should not be published on CurseForge, ModRinth or any similar platform.**<br>

## Features :
- Press V to configure Voice Chat (Simple Voice Chat)
- Hold Alt to build easily (Effortless Structure)
- Craft gliders (Hang Glider) and airplanes (Immersive Aircraft) to get around faster
- Build ships and become a true pirate (Simple Ships)
- Build crazy farms and contraptions (Create + Add-ons)
- Decorate your builds and take the time to cook a nice meal (Another Furniture, Farmer's Delight + Add-ons)
- Become the Airplane! (Do A Barrel Roll, Elytra Trims)
- Be artistic! (Exposure + Polaroid)
- Go on an adventure and explore an unknown world (YUNG's mods, Terralith + Tectonic, Comforts)
- More QoL additions, optimizations and new gameplay features to discover!

## Recommended additions
The following mods are not included in the modpack, but are nice additions for people who don't mind the performance hit.
- Distant Horizons
- Chunky

## Visual changes
Brobin's Vanilla++ also includes Fresh Animations as well as a custom Vanilla Tweaks resource pack.<br>
The Complementary Reimagined and Photon shader packs are also included.
